Frequently Asked Questions

What is ClubLink?
ClubLink is a gateway to connect students to all the clubs and activities going on around school, filling the void of conenctivity between students and clubs.
 How Do I Display My Club?
In order to display your club, you will need to navigate to the forms tab to submit a club. Please keep in mind that the clubs tab is updated weekly so your club may take up to 7 days to display. Additionally, to update your club, simply fill out the form with your club name and the field you desire to change. (You don't have to fill out fields that will remain unchanged) These updates can also take up to 7 days to display on the clubs page.
 Do I Need to Submit Pictures?
No pictures are not necessarily required, but it's preferred to have pictures to allow your clubs page to look as best as can be. If no pictures are submitted, there may be placeholders until pictures are uploaded.
